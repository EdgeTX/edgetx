# Flysky ST16 Hardware Mod for Complete touch Support

This touch screen modification can only be used under the edgetx system and cannot be applied to the flyskyos system.

Adding a touch screen will occupy the wiring of the two rear buttons, thereby affecting the functionality of those two buttons.

## Screen material preparation

Separate the original outer screen from the inner screen

[![Screen_separation](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Screen_separation.png)](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Screen_separation.png)

Handling the touch glass

Remove the inner protective film and the double-sided adhesive.

[![Touching_the_glass_treatment](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Touching_the_glass_treatment.png)](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Touching_the_glass_treatment.png)

Cut off the protruding part of the front protective film and keep the protective film intact.

[![Touching_the_glass_treatment_2](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Touching_the_glass_treatment_2.png)](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Touching_the_glass_treatment_2.png)

[![Wire_connection](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Wire_connection.png)](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Wire_connection.png)

Display screen 1-6 pin：
1-GND
2-RST
3-INT
4-SCL
5-SDA
6-VCC
The touch screen is connected to the display's 1-6 pins via 6-pin connector.

Welding 6-pin and 40-pin connectors.

40pin connector, 0.5mm pitch, with the cover attached below

6pin connector, 0.5mm pitch, upper connection

## Touchscreen assembly

The materials required for screen assembly

[![Screen_assembly_components](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Screen_assembly_components.png)](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Screen_assembly_components.png)

Place the inner screen inside the screen frame, and have the wires pass through the gap.

[![Screen_installation](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Screen_installation.png)](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Screen_installation.png)

Then place the touch glass outside the screen frame, and have the wiring pass through the gap.

[![Touching_glass_installation](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Touching_glass_installation.png)](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Touching_glass_installation.png)

As shown in the picture, open the touch glass. Clean the inner screen and the inner side of the touch glass. Put in the TPU gasket.

[![Install_the_TPU_gasket](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Install_the_TPU_gasket.png)](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Install_the_TPU_gasket.png)

Fasten the screen cover plate

[![Top_cover_installation](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Top_cover_installation.png)](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Top_cover_installation.png)

Connect the 6-pin touch cable to the FPC cable, and then connect the 40-pin cable.

[![FPC_connection](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/FPC_connection.png)](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/FPC_connection.png)

Use tape to fix the fpc to the back of the outer frame.

[![Placement_of_FPC](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Placement_of_FPC.png)](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/Placement_of_FPC.png)

Put the screen assembly back into the remote control and insert the screws to connect the wires.

[![FPC_connector_module](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/FPC_connector_module.png)](../assets/images/hw/flysky-st16/FPC_connector_module.png)
